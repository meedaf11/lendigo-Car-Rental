How Lendigo Works

Whether you're a renter or a car rental agency, Lendigo makes the process seamless and efficient.

1

Sign Up / Log In

Create your account as a user or agency to access platform features.

2

Search & Filter

Use filters like brand, city, price, or agency to find the perfect car.

3

View Car Details

Check specifications, photos, fuel type, transmission, and availability.

4

Book Instantly

Select your dates and submit a reservation request directly to the agency.

5

Track Booking

Monitor booking status (Waiting, Confirmed, Rejected) in your dashboard.

6

Leave a Review

After completing your rental, rate both the car and the agency.
